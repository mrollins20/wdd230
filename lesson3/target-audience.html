Target Audience

Who is our Audience?

    We aim to get our community to support and engage with our local business and government.

Personas

    During the events of a natural disaster, John Doe was impressed with how organized and efficient the chamber was at helping round up help and support with clean up. He now wants to know how he can contribute in the future.

    Jane Doe has been an advocate of Forest preservation for as long as she can remember. After finding seeing most of our policies for reservation care, she decided to run join the member board to have a say in more wild life preservation.

Scenarios

    Scenario 1: A county resident wants to know what a chamber of commerce is all about, and why its important.

    Scenario 2: A business owner wants to know what activities they can participate in.

    Scenario 3: A county resident wants to know who the members of the chamber are and what their responsibilities are.

    Scenario 4: A county resident wants to know what perks are included with being a member.

    Scenario 5: A business owner wants to advertise or promote their business.
