Color Choice Explanation

    The reason for these colors is largely influenced by the psychological tendencies behind the colors. This palatte uses mainly blue, green and grey because of their emphasis on trust, nature, and perfection. I believe that the greys contribute to a cleanliness, while the blues and green add a bit of emphasis to certain elements.
