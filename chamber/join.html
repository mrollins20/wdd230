Membership Information

NP Membership

This membership is for non-profit organizations and there is no fee.

Bronze Membership

This is the lowest priced membership with all the benefits of lower memberships plus ability to advertise on the discovery page.

Silver Membership

This membership includes the benefits of the bronze and non-profit memberships; along with the ability to be seen on the events page and directly communicate with the chamber board.

Gold Membership

Being the highest tier of membership, this bundles all of the benefits that we can offer. Notibly, being able to appear in the spotlights on the main page and contribute more to community projects.
